Specifications

  • 01 Structure & Flooring

    RCC frame structure. Granite tile flooring of branded quality & standardised company on each floor.

  • 02 Electrification & Fire Fighting

    Electrification will be done by Finolex / R.R or equivalent cable & modular switches with ISI standard fittings of branded quality. Dish TV & AC points in shops / offices. Fire fighting norms As per G.D.C.R Norms.

  • 03 Sign Board & Elevation

    Well detailed space for front sign board. Provision of electric point to each sign board. Combined ACP, glass and aluminium fins.

  • 04 Plumbing & Washrooms

    2 wide common washroom on each floor. Well ventilated and designed attached toilet facilities in all offices. Lintel level granamite tile dedo with modern concept.

  • 05 Generator Backup

    Power backup generator for elevators, water pump, common passage lighting, parking.

  • 06 Doors & Windows

    Hand operated galvanized shutters for showroom / shops. Granite marble frame and attractive main door for all offices. All windows are of colour anodized aluminium extruded section.
