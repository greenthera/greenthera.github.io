Specifications

  • 01 Paints & Flooring duis?

    Interior surface of the wall finished with white cement based putty. The outer surface of the wall finished by exterior paint texture and acrylic base water repellent color of branded company. Vitrified/Marble/Granite flooring of branded quality & standardized company on each floor..

  • 02 Electrification & Fire Fighting

    Electrification will be done by Finolex/R.R Cable/Polycab or any equivalent cable & modular switches with ISI standard fitting of branded quality. Fire fighting norms As per G.D.C.R Norms.

  • 03 Lifts & Staircase

    Granite trade & granite riser with granite flooring in a passage with S.S railing with glass. Standard company auto door lifts.

  • 04 Water Supply & Washrooms

    Provision of bore well, underground tank & overhead tank. As per ISI, we make standard quality bath fitting in all washrooms, connection designed fully glazed tiles dado up to lintel level.

  • 05 Generator Backup & Elevation

    Backup Power generator for elevators, escalators, water pump, common passage lighting, parking, etc. Combination of ACP, glass & aluminum fins.

  • 06 Structure, Doors & Windows

    EarthQuake Resistant,R.C.C Structure. Decorative main doors with wooden frames. All other internal doors will be granite frame flush type with enamel paints. Fully glazed powder coated aluminium section sliding windows with natural/granite sill.
