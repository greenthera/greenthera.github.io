Specifications

  • 01 Structure & Flooring

    EarthQuake Resistant RCC structure. Vitrified tiles flooring of standard quality.

  • 02 Electrification & Fire Fighting

    Electrification will be done by Finolex/R.R equivalent cable & modular switches with ISI quality fittings. Fire fighting norms As per G.D.C.R Norms.

  • 03 Lifts & Staircase

    Attractive elevation of glass and aluminium fins. Granite trade and granamite riser with granamite flooring in passage with S.S railing.

  • 04 Water Supply & Washrooms

    Provision of bore well, underground tank & overhead tank. Well ventilated and designed attached toilet facilities in all offices. Lintel level granamite tile dedo with modern concept.

  • 05 Generator Backup & Lifts

    Backup Power generator for elevators, water pump, common passage lighting, parking, etc. Standard company lift.

  • 06 Doors & Windows

    Hand operated galvanized rolling shutters for showrooms / shops. Granite marble frame and attractive main door for last floor only. All windows are of powder coated aluminium extruded sections.
