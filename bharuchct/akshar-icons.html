Specifications

  • 01 Structure & Flooring

    RCC beam column. Earthquake resistence structure as per structural design. Fully vetrified tiles of size 2' x 2' in entire Bungalow and Flat units. Wooden flooring in master bed room in Bungalow unit only.

  • 02 Electrification & Lift

    Concealed electrification with sufficient points. Modular switches and standard MCB for safety. One elevator of 5 passenger in Bungalow units only.

  • 03 Water supply & Toilets

    Designer tiles dedo upto lintel level and Standard fixtures. RO filter will be provided in all residential units. Water supply will be through under ground and over head water tanks.

  • 04 Kitchen

    Black / Gray granite platforms with SS sink and designer tiles dedo upto lintel level.

  • 05 Wall finish & Windows

    Powder coated aluminium section glass window with granite sill. Inside wall with putty finish and outside with acrylic based paints.
